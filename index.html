Frequently asked questions

What exactly is a WCAG 2.1 violation?
WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) 2.1 violations are failures to meet accessibility standards required by the ADA, Section 508, and EN 301 549. Common examples: images without alt text, insufficient color contrast, form fields without labels, or keyboard traps. A violation means the element is failing a specific criterion.
